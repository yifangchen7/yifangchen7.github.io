Research Overview

I’ve worked across both experimental and computational research. On the experimental side: organic synthesis for peptide hydrogels, and inorganic/organometallic nanomaterials. On the computational side: multiscale simulations of protein–protein, protein–peptide, protein–ligand, glycogen–enzyme, and membrane–protein systems. I currently focus on chromatin dynamics using a coarse-grained model implemented in OpenMM within our group.
